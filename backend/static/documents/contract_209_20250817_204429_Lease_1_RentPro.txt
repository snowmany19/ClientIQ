
LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement (the "Lease") is entered into as of August 20, 2024 by and between ProjectCorp, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of California ("Landlord"), and RentPro, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the commercial building located at 1843 Business Street, Suite 892, Los Angeles.

2. TERM
The term of this Lease shall be 2 years commencing on the effective date.

3. RENT
Tenant shall pay monthly rent of $9,317 due on the first day of each month.

4. UTILITIES
Tenant shall be responsible for all utilities included and other utilities as specified in this Lease.

5. MAINTENANCE
Landlord shall be responsible for structural repairs and major systems. Tenant shall be responsible for routine maintenance and cleaning.

6. USE
Tenant shall use the premises solely for business purposes and in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Lease upon 30 days written notice to the other party.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Lease as of the date first above written.
